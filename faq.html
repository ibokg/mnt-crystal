General Questions

What services does MnT Crystal Cleaning offer?
MnT Crystal Cleaning offers a range of cleaning services, including office cleaning, residential cleaning, deep cleaning, and more. Check out our "Services" page for a detailed list.
How do I book a cleaning service with MnT Crystal Cleaning?
Booking is easy! You have two convenient options:

Payment and Billing

What forms of payment does MnT Crystal Cleaning accept?
MnT Crystal Cleaning exclusively accepts online payments. We have streamlined our process to ensure a secure and convenient payment experience for our clients.
Why do you only accept online payments?
Accepting online payments allows us to provide a more efficient and secure service. It eliminates the need for handling cash, streamlines the booking process, and enhances overall convenience for our clients.
What online payment methods are accepted?
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and other secure online payment methods. Our payment system is designed to provide a seamless and hassle-free experience.
Can I pay in cash for my cleaning service?
No, we do not accept cash payments. To ensure the security and efficiency of our services, we exclusively accept online payments.

Discounts and Promotions

Yes! We appreciate your choice in MnT Crystal Cleaning. First-time customers can enjoy a $30 discount on their initial cleaning service.
